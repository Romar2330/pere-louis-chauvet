Our Journey

The journey of PLC's cheer dance was a rich tapestry, intricately woven with dedication, sacrifice, and an indomitable spirit that transcended their MAPEH PETA performance. Each member poured their heart and soul into perfecting every routine, even as they navigated the challenges of occasionally missing the call times.

Their relentless commitment was evident in the countless tears and sweat that marked their grueling practice sessions. Amidst the intense training, the team found solace and joy in their food trips to Rizal Park. These outings were more than just meals; they were moments of laughter and camaraderie, where shared experiences forged an unbreakable bond.

The effort invested in crafting stunning costumes and mastering intricate routines was a testament to their unwavering determination and unity. Through every high and low, they emerged not just as a team but as a family. Each performance became a celebration of their collective perseverance, the beautiful journey they shared, and the friendships that blossomed along the way.

The memories created during this time will forever be cherished, a reminder of the power of teamwork and the joy found in pursuing a shared passion.
