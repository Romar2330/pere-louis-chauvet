About PLC

In the vibrant halls of SPUP, the 2nd science section of grade 10, known as Pere Louis Chauvet, stands as a testament to the beauty of resilience and camaraderie. Guided by the gentle wisdom of their adviser, the beautiful, kind, smart, and loving Ma'am Christina P. Paraggua, these 40 remarkable students defy the label of the "worst science section" with grace and determination.

Each day, they weave a tapestry of fun, genuine bonds that are as strong as they are uplifting. Their intelligence shines brightly, illuminating the path for one another as they navigate the challenges of adolescence. Despite the trials they face, their hearts brim with kindness and compassion, always ready to support a peer in need.

Pere Louis Chauvet is more than just a class; it is a family, united by shared experiences and an unbreakable spirit.

Talent abounds in this extraordinary group, and together they create a safe haven where every voice is heard and every dream is nurtured. The strength of Pere Louis Chauvet lies not only in their academic pursuits but in their ability to lift each other up and face challenges together, inspiring all who have the privilege to know them.
